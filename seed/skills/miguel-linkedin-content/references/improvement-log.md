### 2026-05 — "Most senior engineers are about to discover they're not senior anymore"

**Topic**: Senior engineers who refuse AI vs juniors shipping with Claude/Cursor
**Posting time**: Approximately Tuesday/Wednesday afternoon Lisbon time

**Numbers (48h after publication)**:
- Impressions: 15,416
- Reactions: 42
- Comments: 54
- Engagement rate: ~0.62%
- DMs generated: Unknown but reportedly multiple

**What worked**:
- One-line opener that named a tribe and predicted decline
- "I'll type what nobody wants to:" taboo-breaker
- Concrete contrast (10-year engineer vs 22-year-old with Cursor)
- Pre-empted obvious objections about hallucinations
- Two-path close with question

**What didn't work or surprised me**:
- Comment volume (54) was higher than expected relative to impressions

**One-line lesson**:
- Identity Threat archetype works at high volume on first use with a sharp target

**Should an archetype or constraint be updated**: No — this is the template entry for Identity Threat in `post-archetypes.md`

### 2026-05 — "AI is going to replace engineers in 18 months" (Microsoft Diffusion Report)

**Archetype**: Value Framework (data-driven)
**Topic**: Engineer job market data, AI elasticity argument
**Posting time**: Approximately 2-4 days after the senior engineer post

**Numbers (48h after publication)**:
- Impressions: 3,377
- Reactions: 20
- Comments: 4
- Engagement rate: ~0.71%
- DMs generated: Low

**What worked**:
- Data-driven framing got moderate reach
- Engagement rate was actually slightly higher than the senior engineer post on a per-impression basis

**What didn't work or surprised me**:

- Data-driven posts get viewed and scrolled past; they don't generate arguments

**Should an archetype or constraint be updated**: Yes — `post-archetypes.md` should note that Value Framework posts perform best when they end with a question that forces the reader to take a side

## Looking for patterns

After each batch of 5-10 new entries, ask:

1. **Which archetypes are performing best?** Update the priority order in `post-archetypes.md`.
2. **Which posting times produced the best results?** Update `tactical-execution.md`.
3. **Which topics generated the most DMs from ICP-fit buyers?** This is the leading indicator for content strategy.
4. **Which posts violated `honest-constraints.md` and what happened?** If a post with a forbidden phrase still performed well, the constraint might still be right (long-term brand damage doesn't show up in 48h numbers). If a post violated a constraint and got called out in comments, the constraint is validated.
5. **Are there new patterns that should become their own archetype?** If 3+ posts share a successful structure that isn't in the existing archetypes, add it as a new archetype.

The skill is meant to evolve with the data. After 6 months of entries here, the skill will be significantly more accurate than it is today.
